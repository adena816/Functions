*PURSED LIPS BREATHING*

How does it help?

/

reduce breathlessness by slowing your pace of breathing. This is particularly useful if your shortness of breath is due to anxiety.

How does it work?

/

It helps support open the airways, allowing air to leave the lungs more easily. This creates more room for each next breath.

Steps to follow:

/

→ Sit upright in a chair with the shoulders relaxed.
→ Press your lips together, keeping a small gap between them.
→ Inhale through the nose for a few seconds.
→ Gently exhale through the pursed lips for a count of four.
→ Repeat this pattern a few times.
