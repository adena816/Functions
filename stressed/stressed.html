*HUMMING BEE BREATHING*

How does it help?

/

Research shows that this technique can help reduce your heart rate and feel less irritable or stressed.

How does it work?

/

This yogic humming technique creates an instant sense of calm and is especially soothing around your forehead.

Steps to follow:

/

→ Choose a comfortable seated position.
→ Close your eyes and relax your face.
→ Place your index finger on the tragus cartilage that partially covers your ear canal.
→ Inhale and gently press your finger into the cartilage as you exhale.
→ Keeping your mouth closed, make a loud humming sound.
→ Continue for as long as is comfortable.
