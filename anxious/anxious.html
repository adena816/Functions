*The 4-7-8 BREATHING TECHNIQUE*

How does it help?

/

It decrease anxiety, sleep better, manage food cravings and control emotional responses like anger.

How does it work?

/

It helps regulate the hormone Cortisol, which controls your fight or flight response.

Steps to follow:

/

→ Find somewhere comfortable to sit.
→ Breathe in through your nose to the count of four.
→ Hold the breath to the count of seven.
→ Exhale through your mouth to the count of eight.
